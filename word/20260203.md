## exchange
**中文释义**  
交换；交流；兑换

**English Definition**  
To give something and receive something else in return; to share information or ideas.

**音标**  
/ɪkˈstʃeɪndʒ/

**词性**  
n. / v.

**使用场景**  
- We exchanged ideas during the meeting.  
  我们在会议中交流了想法。  
- You can exchange foreign currency at the bank.  
  你可以在银行兑换外币。
